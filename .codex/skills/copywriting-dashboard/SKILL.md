# Dashboard Copywriting

Write clear, functional, and efficient copy for dashboards and web apps.

## Tone & Style
- Direct and practical
- Neutral, not emotional
- Fast to understand
- No marketing tone

---

## Rules
- Prioritize clarity over style
- Keep labels short and precise
- Avoid unnecessary words
- Avoid storytelling

---

## Common Patterns

### Section Titles
- "Overview"
- "Recent Activity"
- "Team Performance"
- "Pending Tasks"

---

### Table & Data Labels
- "Status"
- "Created Date"
- "Assigned To"
- "Last Updated"

---

### Empty States
- Short and helpful
- Explain what to do next

Example:
- "No projects yet"
- "Create your first project to get started"

---

### Alerts / Messages
- Clear and actionable

Example:
- "Changes saved successfully"
- "Failed to load data"
- "Please try again"

---

### Filters / Actions
- "Filter by Status"
- "Sort by Date"
- "Export Data"

---

## Avoid
- Long sentences
- Marketing language
- Emotional storytelling
- Vague wording

---

## Output Rules
- Return copy only
- Keep it structured if needed
- No explanation

---

## Self-check
- Is this clear instantly?
- Is it too wordy?
- Can user act on this?
- Is tone consistent with dashboard UI?
