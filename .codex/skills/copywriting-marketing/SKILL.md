# Marketing Copywriting

Write compelling but controlled marketing copy for modern websites.

## Tone & Style
- Clear, confident, and human
- Emotionally engaging but not exaggerated
- Premium, calm, and intentional
- Avoid hype and corporate jargon

## Core Principles
- Clarity over cleverness
- Benefits > features
- Specific > generic
- Show outcome, not just capability

---

## Structure

### Hero Section
- Strong headline (non-generic)
- Short supporting line
- 1–2 CTAs

### Section Format
- Eyebrow (optional)
- Title (impactful)
- Description (1–2 lines)
- Optional bullets or CTA

---

## Writing Guidelines

### Headlines
Avoid:
- "We provide solutions"
- "Your trusted partner"

Prefer:
- Outcome-focused
- Transformation-driven
- Clear positioning

---

### Descriptions
- Keep to 1–2 sentences
- Focus on result or benefit
- Avoid repeating headline

---

### CTAs
- Action-driven
- Outcome clear

Good:
- "Start Your Project"
- "Get a Quote"
- "Explore Services"

Avoid:
- "Learn More"
- "Click Here"

---

## Output Rules
- Return structured copy
- No explanation
- No filler text

---

## Self-check
- Is this generic?
- Can it be shorter?
- Is the benefit clear?
- Does it sound human?
- Is tone consistent?
